How We Met

Julie and Dan both lived in the Rodney dorm complex their freshman year at the University of Delaware, but they never crossed paths. The following year, they were both eager to move into the Towers, the infamous on-campus apartments, but were disappointed to find out they were placed in regular dorms again. As it turned out, their new dorms were directly across the hall from one another.

A couple months later, Dan invited Julie on a date to Klondike Kate's on Main Street. Dan ordered meatloaf (which has since become a joke between them) and Julie was so nervous that she only ate half her meal (which has also become a joke between them). On November 17, 2010, Dan officially asked Julie to be his girlfriend underneath a meteor shower on Laird Campus and they've been together ever since.
